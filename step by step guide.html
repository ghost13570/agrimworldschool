Step by step guidelines for Admissions

The School invites applications for admission to class Kindergarten (Nursery) to class XII.

Procedure
Visit the School, Pay Registration Fees, and Collect the Prospectus
Log on to the School portal www.xyz.in and click on the Admissions >Admission form
Admissions form will appear.
Please enter your ward's details and Submit it.
Admission Test details shall be intimated by the admissions office subsequently.
Results of Admission Test shall be individually intimated and Invitation for Interaction with the Parents shall be communicated.
Please visit the school thereafter, Pay the Fees and complete the admission formalities.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR ADMISSION
1. Admission Form duly completed.
2. Photocopy of Birth Certificate
3. One passport photograph each of mother & father.
4. Five passport size photographs of the child.
5. Original Transfer Certificate/School Leaving Certificate duly attested( Grade 2 and above). Results of Admission Test shall be individually intimated and Invitation for Interaction with the Parents shall be communicated.
6. Photocopy of original mark sheet of last examination passed (Only for the examinee age group)
7. a). Medical Certificate to be signed by a certified Medical Practitioner b). Details of allergies and any other chronic ailment. c). A copy of Vaccination Card.
8. Proof of Residence.
